Privacy policy

This web application asks users to connect with their Facebook accounts in order to see the pages they like. When connecting for the first time, they are asked by the Facebook authentication plugin to allow the application to access these information.

Although connecting with Facebook automatically shares the public profile of a user (see Public Profile and Friend List for more information), this application do not use any other information than the Facebook pages the users like. By logging in with their Facebook account and allowing the application to see their likes, users explicitly share these information with the application.

When users participate in our research study, we only store their judgments when assessing the relevance of suggestions.

A web application by the Terrier Team of the University of Glasgow for the SMART FP7 project. Developed by Romain Deveaud.
